Use this service to:

  • consent to the Money and Pensions Service using your information to find your pensions
  • manage what you have consented to
  • consent to another authorised persion viewing your pensions

Before accessing the Find your pensions service, we need you to consent to the processing of your personal data within the service, so that we can find your pensions and you can view them on your dashboard.

The service will use markers/tokens/identifiers, which are collections of digital information to represent you and your pensions. We need your consent to make these markers/tokens/identifiers and send them to the pension providers to find your pension(s).

We will not use your personal information for any other purpose other than finding your pensions and we will not share it with any other parties outside the Find your pensions service.
